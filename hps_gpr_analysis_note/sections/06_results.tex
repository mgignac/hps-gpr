\subsection{2015 analysis}
\label{sec:results-2015}

The completed 2015 study uses the engineering-run invariant-mass histogram over the
\SIrange{20}{130}{MeV} scan region, the 2015 mass-resolution parameterization, and the
published radiative-fraction treatment embedded in the current package. The principal
deliverables are the signal-yield and $\eps^2$ upper-limit bands, complemented by
local/global $p_0$ bookkeeping and representative fit displays.

\subsection{2016 10\% analysis}
\label{sec:results-2016}

The 2016 validation study currently summarized in the package corresponds to the 10\%
subset used for rapid scan development, toy production, and extraction displays. The
statistical treatment is otherwise identical to the 2015 case, with the important
difference that the 2016 resolution model includes the linear-tail extension above
\SI{180}{MeV}.

\subsection{2015+2016 10\% combined upper limit on \texorpdfstring{$\eps^2$}{epsilon2}}
\label{sec:results-combined-1516}

The first completed shared-$\eps^2$ combination joins the 2015 engineering run and the
2016 10\% validation subset through the concatenated blind-window likelihood described
in Section~\ref{sec:combination}. In the overlap region, the combination improves
sensitivity by adding statistically independent information while correctly mapping the
common coupling to dataset-specific signal yields through the conversion factors
$K_d(m)$.
